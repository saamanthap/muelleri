## How to use transrate (for transcriptome assembly QC)

Transrate is already installed on Nibi, meaning you can load it super conveniently as a module. It performs three types of checks: sequence based, read-mapping based and reference based. Reference based is less relevant to me because I don't have a reference transcriptome (I could use the laevis transcriptome but it isn't ideal), but the other two will be helpful.
